About Her

The word 'Ekta', which means unity in English, truly defines her personality. She unites everyone who comes in touch with her. She's a poet when she's extremely happy or sad. Otherwise, she's a mischievous child disguised in a body of a sophisticated adult.


If you know her, you know you can spend hours with her without getting bored. The plus point is that you don't have to speak even. She does all the talking. If you don't know her, don't fret, Ekta knows you. Ekta knows "EVERYONE". You might have applied extreme privacy settings on your social media account, but Ekta can tell which cafe you went last week or who your latest crush is.

When I say she has a great future being a detective, I'm not joking. Her friends have exploited her great stalking skills to get information about private and not-so-private life of others. Do contact her if you have been blocked by your ex and want to know what he/she is upto now.

Voracious Eater

There's nothing in the world of food that Ekta doesn't like. If you don't want to eat your tiffin someday and are afraid of your mom too, don't worry, Ekta is there for you. Some of her favourite food items are: Amritsari Kulcha with Raita, Soya Masala Chap and Naan, Choley Bhature with Lassi and Pyaz, and anything that she hasn't paid for.

Elegant Poser

Your friendship level with Ekta is directly proportional to your phone's camera quality. And if you want to enhance your photography skills, Ekta is there for you to give infinte poses.
